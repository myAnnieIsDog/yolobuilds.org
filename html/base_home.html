Search by location to view all public records that are available for an address or APN.

Building issues all building permits including construction, demolition, change of use, electrical, mechacnical, plumbing, grading, fire, flood, and pool/spa.

Business Licenses issues all business licenses for businesses operating in the unincorporated portions of Yolo County.

Contact the Yolo County Recorder to record a fictitious business name (DBA).

Code Enforcement investigates reported code violations and works with the appropriate individuals and agencies to abate violations.

Environmental Health issues permits for wells, septic systems, solid waste, hazardous materials, and consumer protection (food service, tatoos, piercing, etc.).

Integrated Waste Management enforces CALGreen recycling requirements.

Planning issues both ministerial reviews and discretionary land use permits.

Public Works issues encroachment permits, transportation permits, and county surveyor requests.
